You can find the model checkpoints in this Google Drive "checkpoints" folder, organized into three subfolders for the following:

1. Simple CNN Model: Includes weight files for the Simple CNN model.
2. Advanced CNN Model: Includes weight files for the Advanced CNN model.
3. VGG 19 Model: Includes weight files for the VGG 19 model.

Each subfolder is labeled with a timestamp, indicating when it was created. To access and download the model checkpoints, follow the link below:

https://drive.google.com/drive/folders/1b_UcwpEHz97HPT_BtKtE8l0KXQT4Hete?usp=share_link
